Nearest Airports to Historical Monuments

Plan your trip with ease by finding the nearest airports to your favorite historical sites in India.

Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Nearest Airport: Agra Airport (AGR)

Distance: 13 km

Transportation: Taxis, buses, rental cars

Travel Tips: Best visited during the cooler months from October to March.

Lotus Temple

Location: Delhi

Nearest Airport: Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL)

Distance: 20 km

Transportation: Metro, taxis, buses

Travel Tips: Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds.

Qutub Minar

Location: Delhi

Nearest Airport: Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL)

Distance: 13 km

Transportation: Metro, taxis, buses

Travel Tips: Combine with a visit to the nearby Mehrauli Archaeological Park.
